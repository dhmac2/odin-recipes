Scrambled Eggs in a Mug

For delicious scrambled eggs, microwave one egg in a cup with a bit of milk and pepper for each person in your family. It's quick and easy, and there will be no messy pan to clean.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk egg in a microwave-safe mug. Whisk in milk until well blended and light yellow in color
  2. microwave on high until cooked through and fluffy, about 90 seconds. Season with pepper and serve.
